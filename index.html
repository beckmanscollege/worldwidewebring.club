World Wide Webring

World Wide Webring is an assignment, part of the course ‘Interactive design #1’, tought for the first year students at Visual communication at Beckmans College of Design with senior lecturer in digital design Peter Ström.

The students each pick a Wikipedia article of interest based on a yearly theme and create a single-serving site with the article as its starting point. Each student SSS is connected to the other students as a webring.
